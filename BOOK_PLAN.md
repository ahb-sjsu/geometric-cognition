## Running Example: Maya Chen

Maya Chen is a cognitive scientist studying both human and artificial reasoning. She uses the Measuring AGI benchmarks to probe AI systems and functional MRI to probe human brains. Her research question: do human and artificial cognition share geometric structure? Each chapter follows her as she discovers another piece of the cognitive manifold.
